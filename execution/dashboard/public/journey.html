04 — Post-Call Emails

5 AI-Generated Email Types

Every email is personalised by Claude using the call transcript. Each type has a distinct tone, content strategy, and included elements.

Nurture Email

Trigger: FOLLOW_UP (no date)
  • Personalised opening from call summary
  • Relevant career fact with urgency context
  • Upcoming webinar details + link
  • Brochure link (bit.ly/CBA-Brochure2026)
  • "Looking forward to having you onboard" close
  • Encouragement to pursue cloud career

Nurture + Scheduled

Trigger: FOLLOW_UP + follow_up_date
  • Everything from nurture email
  • References the scheduled follow-up date
  • "Looking forward to our chat on [date]"
  • Suggests reviewing brochure before next call
  • Webinar as pre-follow-up activity

Door-Open Email

Trigger: DECLINED
  • Genuine thanks for their time
  • Respects the decision — zero pressure
  • One career fact positioned casually
  • Brochure "for reference if you revisit"
  • No webinar push
  • "If things change, we're here"

Thank-You Email

Trigger: NOT_QUALIFIED
  • Polite, brief (under 100 words)
  • Thanks for taking the time
  • Wishes them well on their journey
  • No brochure, no webinar, no programme push
  • No career facts or urgency

Missed-Call Email

Trigger: NO_ANSWER / VOICEMAIL / BUSY
  • "Tried to reach you today" — light, friendly
  • Brief Cloudboosta intro + why we called
  • Relevant career fact for curiosity
  • Upcoming webinar as no-commitment option
  • Brochure link + Cal.com booking link
  • Under 200 words

Payment Email

Trigger: COMMITTED
  • Programme-specific bank details
  • Currency-aware (GBP/USD/EUR/NGN)
  • Payment reference generated
  • Instalment options if applicable
  • Next steps: proof of payment instructions
  • Navy blue branded HTML template

07 — Cal.com Booking

Booking Flow with Double-Booking Prevention

Two layers of protection: an IF-node check for the obvious case, and an atomic PATCH guard for race conditions.
